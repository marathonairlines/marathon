Marathon Miles Loyalty Program

Get to Know Marathon Miles

With Marathon Miles, every trip and purchase can bring you to the next place on your travel wish list. Earn miles for flying and everyday activities, then use miles towrad travel on any Marathon Air Lines flight with no blackout dates or for rewards.

Earn When You Fly

As a Marathon Miles customer, you'll earn 3 miles for every dollar you spend. So when you pay a higher price, you're rewarded with more miles.

How to Earn on Flights:

  • Book a flight - it's that easy.

  • Achieve Trophy Status and earn even more - up to 12 miles for every dollar spent.

Earn More with Trophy Status

You deserve to be rewarded for you loyalty, so customers earn more miles at each Trophy Tier. The amount you'll earn is based on your status at the time of your travel.

Bronze

Earn 5 miles for every dollar you spend on your flight, and receive access to priority boarding and waive same-day change fees.

Silver

Earn 8 miles for every dollar you spend on your flight, and receive access to Bronze promotions as well as Marathon+ seating.

Gold

Earn 12 miles for every dollar you spend on your flight, and receive access to Silver promotions as well as free in-flight menu items.
